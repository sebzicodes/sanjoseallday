You are on First Street between Santa Clara and San Fernando Street

While walking down first street you see many businesses and tram heading northbound on a track. You see Good karma, a vegan friendly eatery with great craft beers to your west. On the west side of the street there is also a subway, an ice cream sandwich shop called CREAM, then a street that branches off about halfway down the block. south of the street branching off to the west theres Fifty-Five South Bar. Peering inside of fifty-five south you see many unique liqours.

On the east side of the street you see an alleyway going east, a cookie bar called Insomnia, a restaurant called Nomkai eatery, and then a few bars in succession. The bars you see are The Temple bar and lounge which seems upon first glace like a upscale club, the Fuze lounge which is very lit up and musical, Still OG and Alter Ego which looks like a hule in the wall but you have a feeling theres more than meets the eye, and Paper Planes which has a line around the block.
