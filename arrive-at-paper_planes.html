You have arrived at Paper Planes

Walking up to the bar you see a long line to enter the bar. The security guards are checking patrons IDs at the door. Everyone waiting looks very excited to get in and have a drink. As you step over the tram track towards the back of the line you hear a horn whistle from the tram approaching. You step aside the tracks to allow the tram passage.

Everything around you seems very lively. You notice the crowd looks to be a lot of younger people with trending fashion sense. Some people in line are talking about their college classes, and some are talking about how incredible the drink was that they had last weekend at Paper Planes. As the line shortens and you near the door you begin to smell something amazing. A few minutes ago while you were waiting and socializing in line, a few hot dog stands have set up all along the block offering drunk bar goers a delicious reprieve from hunger.

The line was long but you find that time just flies by as you converse with the fellow bar goers waiting with you and now youre getting your ID checked by a stern yet professional security gaurd. After he closely inspects your ID he says, "Welcome to Paper Planes! Come on in and enjoy our one of a kind cocktails!"
